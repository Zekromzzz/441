Today is Arbor Day

Arbor Day is a festival that promotes the protection of trees in accordance with legal regulations, and organizes and mobilizes the masses to actively participate in activities related to afforestation.

Our action

in school

On the occasion of the 46th Arbor Day, Ningbo City Vocational and Technical College launched the "Gathering City Campus Emotions, Planting Beautiful Forests Together" activity.

Using a shovel to shovel soil, supporting trees and seedlings, watering and cultivating soil

Origin of Festivals

The origin of Arbor Day can be traced back to ancient tree planting traditions. In ancient China, people had a consciousness of afforestation and environmental protection.
